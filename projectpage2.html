Late Shabbat
In 1999, Rabbi Mintz developed the Late Shabbat Project which became the largest young adult Jewish program in the country. Beginning with a late night shabbat service and evolving into a program that has inspired and engaged thousands of Jews in their 20's and 30's in the Bay Area, the Late Shabbat has given birth to programs around the country, hundreds of marriages, Jewish babies and continues to transform young Jews' lives.
